Things to take into consideration

  1. Budget: What's your budget? Do you have money for tickets there and back, a hotel if needed, and access money for shopping and eating?
  2. Health: Are you ill or injured? whether you are or are not can impact your experience on your trip.
  3. Safety: Check for any crime rates or hazards there might be at your destination before you get there. Additionally, make sure you know what belongings you're bringing and keeping them in a safe place.
  4. Culture Customs: Become familiar with any local etiquette, traditions, and laws to avoid any misunderstandings with locals.
  5. Visa Requirements: Before doing anything, you need to ensure you have the necessary visas/permits for your destination
  6. Currency: Familiarize yourself with the local currencyy as well as the exchange rates to further maintain your budget
  7. Booking Hotels: Make sure you find a proper, safe hotel wihtin your budget before leaving home
