Things to take into consideration:

  1. Budget:

    What's your budget? Do you have money for tickets there and back, a hotel if needed, and access money for shopping and eating?

  2. Health:

    Are you ill or injured? whether you are or are not can impact your experience on your trip.

  3. Safety:

    Check for any crime rates or hazards there might be at your destination before you get there. Additionally, make sure you know what belongings you're bringing and keeping them in a safe place.

  4. Culture Customs:

    Become familiar with any local etiquette, traditions, and laws to avoid any misunderstandings with locals.

  5. Visa Requirements:

    Before doing anything, you need to ensure you have the necessary visas/permits for your destination

  6. Currency:

    Familiarize yourself with the local currencyy as well as the exchange rates to further maintain your budget

  7. Booking Hotels:

    Make sure you find a proper, safe hotel wihtin your budget before leaving home

  8. Staying Informed:


    9. Keep track of what is happening in the area you're traveling to. Since you are not from the area, there may be different precautions.

  9. Packing:


    10. Make sure you pack a little over the exact amount you know you will need at your destination, but don't overpack!

  10. Language:


    11. Familiarize yourself with the official language of where you are visiting
